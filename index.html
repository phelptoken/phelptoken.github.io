Solutions

Bringing crypto payments to the masses:

Through our PHELP TOKEN. Running on the BEP20 platform, PHELP is fast, low in transaction fees, and most importantly - secure.

PHELP P2P Marketplace

Our Marketplace will be a hub where users can buy, sell, and trade every day items using cryptocurrency. PHELP Token will be the primary currency, however we plan to integrate other tokens in the future.

PHELP Point-of-Sale

By integrating our Blockchain into popular e-commerce and shopping cart platforms, we aim to grant users the ability to easily complete their everyday purchases using their crypto currency balances.

PHELP Swap

Users will be able to swap or exchange among their crypto assets. For purposes of growth, this will initially be specific to BSC.

Upon launch of the PHELP Blockchain, PHELP Swap will bridge all known blockchains into its protocol powered and be powered by PHELP Token.

PHELP Vault

PHELP Vault will be a platform with the abilities of Staking, Time-Locking, and Lending. This will be BSC exclusive during our infancy - but the future PHELP Vault will be powered by the PHELP Blockchain and will support modern tokenomics like reflection rewards.
